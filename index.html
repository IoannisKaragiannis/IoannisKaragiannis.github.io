2019

  • During Mobile World Congress 2019 my team demonstrated how 5G allows mixed reality to meet with haptics and spatial audio to enable an immersive communication. Visitors, sharing a common virtual scene that was blended convincingly with the real world, could, in real-time, see, touch, and hear the virtual objects of that scene as if they were real. The epitome of immersiveness and telepresence was achieved when the users were touching the same virtual object simultaneously. BBC Click covered our demo and a video is available here.
